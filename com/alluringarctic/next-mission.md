### Greenland to Europe ###

*[See full screen](//umap.openstreetmap.fr/en/map/alluringarctic_1389096?scaleControl=false&miniMap=false&scrollWheelZoom=true&zoomControl=true&editMode=disabled&moreControl=true&searchControl=null&tilelayersControl=null&embedControl=null&datalayersControl=true&onLoadPanel=none&captionBar=false&captionMenus=true)*

In summer 2026 we’ll sail *s/v Lumi* from Greenland to Europe via Iceland and Faroes. The most challenging part of this trip is around the southern tip of Greenland where ice and difficult weather conditions are common.

During this we’ll collect marine litter data for the [Coastal Marine Litter Observatory](https://cmlo.scidrones.com/) using [drone photogrammetry](https://youtu.be/nPJbRu10b3A?si=TBjUa1lk3tCQI3t6&t=989).
